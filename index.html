Amex Predator

Amex presents the Predator, a pinnacle of innovation and safety. Engineered for peak performance, this helmet combines cutting-edge aerodynamics with unmatched protection to give you the ultimate riding experience.

Key Features & Benefits

Advanced Impact Protection

Our multi-density EPS liner and reinforced composite shell absorb and disperse impact energy, providing superior head protection in all conditions.

Superior Aerodynamics

Designed in a wind tunnel, the sleek shell minimizes drag and lift, reducing rider fatigue and ensuring stability at high speeds.

Dynamic Ventilation System

Stay cool and comfortable with our strategically placed vents and exhaust ports that pull fresh air in and push hot air out, preventing fogging and heat buildup.

Quick-Release Visor

A tool-free visor change system allows for quick and easy swaps, while the anti-scratch and anti-fog coating ensures clear vision on every ride.
